# **App Name**: StressWise

## Core Features:

- Sentiment Input: Allows users to input their current feelings and problems they are facing in their life.
- Quick Questionnaire: Presents a series of quick questions to gauge the user's current state of mind and stress triggers.
- AI Stress Level Detection Tool: Leverages AI to analyze the sentiment input and questionnaire responses to detect the user's stress level and identify key stressors; this may involve a reasoning component where the tool decides which inputs are most important to incorporate into the final assessment.
- Personalized Advice: Provides personalized advice and resources based on the detected stress level.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Calm blue (#64B5F6) to promote relaxation and trust.
- Background color: Light, desaturated blue (#E3F2FD) for a serene environment.
- Accent color: Soft green (#A5D6A7) for positive reinforcement and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' a humanist sans-serif that balances modernity and warmth for readability.
- Use calming and supportive icons related to mental health and stress management.
- Subtle animations when displaying advice and progress.
